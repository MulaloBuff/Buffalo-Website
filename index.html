Know About Us

Buffalo specialises in eCommerce Fulfilment, International Logistics and Courier Services in South Africa and the broader African continent.

eCommerce Fulfilment

Buffalo International excels in eCommerce fulfillment by efficiently managing order processing, inventory, and warehousing. We ensure timely and accurate picking, packing, and shipping of products, coupled with robust returns management to enhance customer satisfaction. Our services are tailored to meet the specific needs of online retailers

International Logistics

In international logistics, Buffalo International offers comprehensive solutions including freight forwarding, customs brokerage, and supply chain management. We facilitate compliant cross-border shipments through multi-modal transport options, optimizing both delivery times and costs.

Courier Services

Buffalo International provides reliable courier services both domestically and internationally. We offer fast, priority shipping options and tracking systems, where customers can monitor their packages in real-time. Our services cater to various delivery needs,from standard shipments to specialized handling of fragile or high-value items.
